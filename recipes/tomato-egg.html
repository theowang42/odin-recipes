Tomato Egg

Tomato egg stir-fry is fast, forgiving, and deeply comforting. The eggs stay soft, the tomatoes become saucy, and the final dish lands between sweet, savory, and bright.

Time15 min
Serves2
LevelEasy
HeatMild

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 medium tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 1 scallion, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon light soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup, optional for extra body
  • Salt and white pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons neutral oil
Tip: Remove the eggs while still slightly underdone. They finish cooking when returned to the pan.

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and white pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet. Add eggs and gently scramble until just set, then remove.
  3. Add tomatoes to the same pan and cook until they start releasing juice.
  4. Season with sugar, soy sauce, and ketchup if using. Simmer until the tomato juices thicken slightly.
  5. Return the eggs to the pan and fold them through the tomato sauce.
  6. Finish with scallions and adjust salt to taste. Serve with steamed rice.
