Tomato and Egg Stir-Fry

Description

Tomato and egg stir-fry is a quick Chinese home dish with soft scrambled eggs and juicy tomatoes. It tastes slightly sweet, lightly savory, and comforting without needing many ingredients.

The key is cooking the eggs first and removing them while they are still tender. The tomatoes are then cooked down into a light sauce before the eggs return to the pan.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 medium tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 scallion, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, plus more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon light soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon ketchup for a brighter tomato flavor

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs with a small pinch of salt until the yolks and whites are fully combined.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add eggs and scramble until just set, then remove.
  3. Add the remaining oil and the scallion whites. Stir briefly until fragrant.
  4. Add tomatoes, sugar, salt, soy sauce, and water. Cook until the tomatoes release juice and soften.
  5. Return the eggs to the pan and fold them gently into the tomato sauce.
  6. Cook for another 30 seconds, then taste and adjust salt if needed.
  7. Garnish with scallion greens and serve with rice.
