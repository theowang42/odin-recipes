Scallion Oil Noodles

Description

Scallion oil noodles are simple, aromatic noodles tossed with soy sauce and slowly infused scallion oil. The dish is minimal, but the flavor is deep because the scallions are fried until sweet and golden.

This recipe is useful when you want something fast but still cooked with intention. The sauce can be prepared while the noodles boil, making it a good weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 200 g wheat noodles
  • 5 scallions, cut into long pieces
  • 3 tablespoons neutral cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoon light soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Optional: toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the neutral oil in a small pan over medium-low heat.
  3. Add scallion pieces and fry slowly until they turn golden and crisp around the edges.
  4. Lower the heat and stir in light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, sugar, and sesame oil.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to a bowl and pour the scallion oil sauce over them.
  6. Toss until every strand is evenly coated.
  7. Top with fried scallions and sesame seeds, then serve immediately.
